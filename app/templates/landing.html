TrueHair AI Hairstyle Visualization Study

A research study from Colby College — Department of Computer Science (CS422)

What this is

TrueHair AI is a research prototype that uses generative AI to show you what you might look like with different hairstyles. You'll upload a front-facing photo, choose a hairstyle from a catalog, and see an AI-generated visualization of yourself with that style.

Get Started — Read Consent

What you'll do

1. Consent

Read and agree to an informed consent disclosure.

2. Upload

Upload a front-facing photo of yourself.

3. Choose

Browse a catalog of hairstyles and select one to try.

4. Visualize

View the AI-generated visualization and rate it.

Before you begin

Participation is voluntary, anonymous, and open to adults (18+). Full details about the study, including how your data is handled, are on the consent page.
